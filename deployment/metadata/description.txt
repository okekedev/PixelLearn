Make learning fun with PixelLearn - the adaptive educational app for kids.

PixelLearn helps children build essential skills in math, grammar, and memory through engaging quizzes and games. With adaptive difficulty that grows with your child, every session is perfectly challenging.

SUBJECTS

• Math
Practice addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and more. Questions adapt from basic counting to advanced problem solving across 65 difficulty levels.

• Grammar
Build language skills with spelling, vocabulary, sentence structure, and punctuation exercises. Perfect for developing strong reading and writing foundations.

• Memory
Train focus and recall with the classic card matching game. Choose from 7 fun emoji themes including fruits, animals, vehicles, nature, sports, and food.

ADAPTIVE LEARNING

PixelLearn adjusts to your child's skill level automatically. Get two answers right in a row and level up. Make a mistake and practice at an easier level. This keeps kids engaged without frustration.

MULTIPLAYER MODE

Turn learning into family game time! Up to 4 players can compete head-to-head on the same device. Take turns answering questions or matching cards. Perfect for siblings, friends, or parent-child learning sessions.

• Pass-and-play multiplayer for 2-4 players
• Individual score tracking
• Each player has their own difficulty level
• Choose any subject for multiplayer games

MEMORY GAME FEATURES

• 6 card count options: 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, or 64 cards
• 7 emoji themes to choose from
• Move counter to track your progress
• Solo play tracks moves (not saved - just for fun!)
• Multiplayer tracks pairs found per player

PROFILES

Create profiles for each child to track their individual progress. Switch between profiles easily from the home screen. Each profile maintains separate levels for all subjects.

• Track correct answers
• Track multiplayer wins
• Individual progress per subject
• Custom avatars

PRIVACY FOCUSED

PixelLearn works completely offline. No accounts, no ads, no data collection, no internet required. Your child's learning stays on your device.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to PixelLearn ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially when it comes to children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

PixelLearn is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your children. Specifically:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or collect analytics data.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  • No Advertisements: The app contains no ads and does not integrate with any advertising networks.

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use, share, or sell any of your data.

Data Storage

All user data, including profiles, scores, and progress, is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to any servers. The data includes:

  • Profile names (chosen by you)
  • Correct answer counts
  • Win counts
  • Current difficulty levels per subject

This data is stored solely to provide the app's functionality and is fully under your control.

Offline Functionality

PixelLearn works completely offline. No internet connection is required to use any features of the app.

Permissions

PixelLearn does not request any special device permissions. The app does not access your photos, camera, microphone, location, or contacts.

Children's Privacy

PixelLearn is designed for children and families. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
